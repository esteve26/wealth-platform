# Purpose

This document defines the philosophy behind the Wealth Platform.

It explains **why** the platform exists, how decisions should be taken and which values must remain constant throughout the lifetime of the product.

This document guides product, engineering and AI decisions.

---

# Vision of Wealth

The Wealth Platform does not define wealth as money alone.

Wealth is the complete set of financial and non-financial assets, liabilities, goals and strategies that describe a person's financial position and future.

The platform must provide a complete, trustworthy and explainable view of that wealth.

---

# Core Philosophy

## Trust before Intelligence

Users must trust every number before they trust any recommendation.

No recommendation is valuable if its underlying data cannot be explained.

---

## Automation before Manual Work

Whenever a reliable connector exists, automation must be preferred.

Manual operations are a fallback mechanism, not the primary workflow.

---

## Explain before Recommend

Every recommendation must explain:

- Why it exists.
- Which facts generated it.
- Which business rules were applied.
- Which alternatives exist.

---

## Financial Engine is the Source of Truth

The Financial Engine is responsible for every financial calculation.

AI never replaces deterministic calculations.

AI consumes verified outputs produced by the Financial Engine.

---

## AI as a Financial Copilot

AI is an assistant.

It helps users understand their financial situation.

It never replaces user judgement.

It never performs regulated financial advice within the initial product scope.

---

## Portfolio First

The platform analyses wealth primarily through portfolios.

Individual assets are important, but decisions should always consider the portfolio as a whole.

---

## Historical Integrity

Historical financial information is preserved.

Entities are archived rather than deleted whenever possible.

History is a strategic asset.

---

## Progressive Disclosure

The platform should remain simple for beginners while allowing advanced users to access deeper analysis.

Complexity should be revealed gradually.

---

## Configuration over Complexity

Users should configure the platform through guided options.

Unlimited customization is not an objective.

The product should remain understandable and maintainable.

---

## Long-term Product

Every architectural and product decision should favour sustainability over short-term convenience.

The Wealth Platform is designed to evolve for many years.

---

# Product Promise

The Wealth Platform promises:

- trustworthy financial information;
- deterministic calculations;
- explainable intelligence;
- transparent recommendations;
- complete ownership of user data;
- continuous evolution.

---

# Definition of Success

The platform succeeds when users:

- stop using spreadsheets as their primary financial tool;
- trust the platform with their financial overview;
- understand why their wealth evolves;
- make better informed financial decisions.
